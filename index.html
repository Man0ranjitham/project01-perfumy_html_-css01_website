Parfum Floral perfumes feature fragrant flowers like rose, jasmine, and lavender. They're fresh, feminine, and perfect for spring and summer. Delicate and charming, they evoke feelings of romance and beauty.

Citrus perfumes are invigorating and uplifting, with top notes of lemon, orange, and grapefruit. They're perfect for daytime wear, providing a refreshing and energizing scent that's sure to boost your mood.

Oriental perfumes are rich and exotic, featuring notes of amber, vanilla, and musk. They're seductive and alluring, perfect for evening wear. Complex and sophisticated, they evoke feelings of luxury and glamour.
